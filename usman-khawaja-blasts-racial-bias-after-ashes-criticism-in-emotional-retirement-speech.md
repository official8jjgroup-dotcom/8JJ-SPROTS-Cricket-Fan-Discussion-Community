# [Usman Khawaja Blasts Racial Bias After Ashes Criticism in Emotional Retirement Speech](https://8jjsports.com/usman-khawaja-blasts/)

Australian cricket star Usman Khawaja has openly addressed the racial bias and discrimination he faced throughout his career, following criticism during the Ashes series over his back injury. Khawaja, who recently announced his retirement from international cricket after the fifth Ashes Test against England, revealed that the scrutiny he faced was not merely about his performance, but deeply personal and tied to racial stereotypes. His retirement press conference became a platform to highlight longstanding issues of racial inequality in Australian cricket.
