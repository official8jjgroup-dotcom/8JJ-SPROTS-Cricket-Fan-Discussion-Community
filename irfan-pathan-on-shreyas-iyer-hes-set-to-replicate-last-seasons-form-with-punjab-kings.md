# Irfan Pathan on Shreyas Iyer: “He’s Set to Replicate Last Season’s Form with Punjab Kings”

Former India all‑rounder **Irfan Pathan** has expressed confidence that **Shreyas Iyer** can replicate his remarkable form from the **2025 Indian Premier League (IPL)** while leading the **Punjab Kings** in the 2026 season. Iyer enters the campaign after finishing as PBKS’s top run‑scorer in 2025 with **604 runs in 17 innings at an average of 50.33 and a strike rate of 175.07**, including six half‑centuries — a performance that has raised expectations for the upcoming IPL. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Pathan, speaking on JioHotstar’s *Game Plan – Know Your Team*, described Iyer as a **proven cricketer** whose experience and calm temperament make him well‑placed to lead Punjab Kings again. He noted that Iyer’s leadership and batting performances have been key to PBKS’s competitiveness in recent seasons, suggesting that the batter’s success isn’t solely dependent on IPL performances. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Leadership and Tactical Balance

Under Iyer’s captaincy, the Punjab Kings aim to blend tactical insight with explosive batting. Pathan highlighted the leadership synergy between Iyer and head coach **Ricky Ponting**, noting their collective ability to motivate the squad and maintain team balance. Iyer’s calm approach in high‑pressure situations, such as his match‑turning 87* last season, underscores his leadership qualities and strategic diligence. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

## Firepower in the Batting Line‑Up

Iyer anchors a strong top order alongside **Prabhsimran Singh** and **Priyansh Arya**, both of whom enjoyed impressive IPL campaigns last season. Singh scored 549 runs in 17 innings at a brisk strike rate, while Arya compiled 475 runs with a century and two fifties. This top‑order trio provides PBKS with stability and scoring depth, allowing Iyer to manage the innings strategically while exploiting scoring opportunities. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

The middle order also features a balanced mix of Indian talent and overseas finishers such as **Marcus Stoinis** and **Mitch Owen**, giving PBKS the batting depth needed to compete in diverse match situations. Pathan emphasised the importance of this blend of experience and emerging talent for consistent performances throughout a long tournament. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

## A Well‑Rounded Bowling Unit

Beyond batting, Iyer will rely on a pace attack led by **Arshdeep Singh** and **Marco Jansen**, supported by seam options such as **Azmatullah Omarzai, Xavier Bartlett, Vyshak Vijaykumar,** and **Ben Dwarshius**. Pathan praised the bowling unit’s depth and variety, highlighting how effective bowling rotation and smart field placements are crucial in T20 cricket. The combination of pace, bounce, and tactical execution positions PBKS as a more complete unit. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

## Looking Ahead: IPL 2026

As the Punjab Kings prepare to kick off their **IPL 2026 season on March 31**, Shreyas Iyer enters the campaign with the weight of expectation but also with the confidence of a proven performer. Pathan concluded that if Iyer continues his growth as both a batter and leader, he could further solidify his place in India’s T20I plans while driving PBKS to a strong showing in the tournament. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
