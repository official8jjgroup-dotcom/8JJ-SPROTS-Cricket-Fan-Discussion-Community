# C[ourtney Walsh Joins Zimbabwe as Bowling Consultant Ahead of T20 World Cup 2026](https://8jjsports.com/courtney-walsh-joins-zimbabwe/)

---

**Zimbabwe Cricket** has taken a major step in preparing for the **T20 World Cup 2026** by appointing legendary **West Indies pacer Courtney Walsh** as the team’s **bowling consultant**.  

Walsh, renowned for his remarkable international cricket career, brings **unmatched expertise and experience** to Zimbabwe’s squad. The team will compete in **Group B** alongside **Australia, Ireland, Oman, and Sri Lanka**, aiming to make a strong impact in the tournament.  

His appointment is expected to enhance the team’s bowling strategy and provide crucial guidance to young pacers ahead of one of cricket’s biggest events.
