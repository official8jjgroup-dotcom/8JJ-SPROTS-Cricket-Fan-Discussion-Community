# [New Zealand and South Africa Begin T20I Series](https://8jjsports.com/new-zealand-and-south-africa-begin-t20i-series/)

**New Zealand and South Africa** have begun a **five‑match T20 International (T20I) series** in New Zealand as both teams reset and rebuild following recent global tournaments. The series officially got underway on **March 15, 2026, at the Bay Oval in Mount Maunganui**, where the first game of the tour saw **South Africa secure a seven‑wicket win over New Zealand** with a strong bowling performance and efficient chase.

The tour features a mix of experienced internationals and new faces for both sides, as selectors manage workloads ahead of the upcoming franchise cricket season and future ICC events. New Zealand captain **Mitchell Santner** is leading the side in the first three T20Is, with **Tom Latham** set to take over for the final two matches.

In addition to the men’s fixtures, the women’s teams are also competing in a parallel T20I series, with **New Zealand Women posting a convincing 80‑run victory over South Africa Women** in the opening match — highlighted by strong performances from Amelia Kerr and Georgia Plimmer.

The five‑match T20I series continues across major New Zealand venues including Hamilton, Auckland, Wellington and Christchurch, with both sides aiming to gain momentum and showcase emerging talent as they look ahead to future international challenges.
