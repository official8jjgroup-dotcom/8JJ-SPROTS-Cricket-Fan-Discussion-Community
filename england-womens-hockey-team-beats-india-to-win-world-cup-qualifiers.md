# [England Women’s Hockey Team Beats India to Win World Cup Qualifiers](https://8jj.com/england-womens-hockey-team-beats-india-to-win-world-cup/)

The **England women’s hockey team** claimed the **FIH Women’s Hockey World Cup Qualifiers title** with a **2–0 victory over hosts India** in the final at the GMC Balayogi Hockey Ground in **Hyderabad, India**. England took control through a penalty corner from **Grace Balsdon** and later doubled the lead with a strike from **Elizabeth Neal** to secure the win.

India showed spirited play and earned several chances, but were unable to convert them into goals against England’s resolute defensive performance. Despite the defeat, India had already secured **qualification for the FIH Hockey Women’s World Cup 2026** earlier in the tournament.{index=1}

England’s triumph in the qualifier not only gave them the trophy but also provided valuable momentum and confidence heading into the **World Cup later this year**. The victory highlighted England’s tactical discipline and composure under pressure.
