KKR’s marquee signing **Cameron Green** is currently **unable to bowl due to a lower back injury**. ### 3. Pace Bowling Injuries  
KKR’s pace bowling resources have been hit hard by injuries prior to the season, with key quicks ruled out or missing initial matches. This has forced the team to rethink their strategy and rely on less experienced options. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

### 5. Pressure on Captaincy and Strategy
