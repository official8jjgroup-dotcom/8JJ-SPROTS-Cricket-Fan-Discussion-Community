# [Quinten Timber Joins Marseille After Feyenoord Success](https://8jj.com/quinten-timber-marseille/)

**Quinten Timber** has officially joined **Marseille** after a successful stint at **Feyenoord**, marking a major step in his professional football career.  

The Netherlands midfielder, twin brother of **Arsenal defender Jurrien Timber**, has signed with the French **Ligue 1** club as he looks to **expand his impact** at a higher level of European football. Fans will be watching closely to see how he adapts to a new league and team environment.
