# [Bangladesh Replaced by Scotland in T20 World Cup 2026 After Security Standoff](https://8jjsports.com/bangladesh-replace-by-scotland-in-t20-world-cup-2026/)

Bangladesh have officially been replaced by Scotland in the ICC T20 World Cup 2026 following their refusal to travel to India for the tournament. The decision came after more than three weeks of tense discussions between the Bangladesh Cricket Board (BCB) and the International Cricket Council (ICC), with security concerns and scheduling disputes dominating the talks.
