# [Max Dowman Becomes Youngest Premier League Goal Scorer](https://8jj.com/max-dowman-becomes-youngest-premier-league-goal-scorer/)

Arsenal teenager **Max Dowman** etched his name into **Premier League history** on **14 March 2026** by becoming the **youngest ever goalscorer in the competition** at just **16 years and 73 days old**. Dowman’s historic strike came in stoppage time during Arsenal’s **2-0 victory over Everton** at the Emirates Stadium, sealing the win and sparking widespread celebration among fans and pundits alike.

The goal was the result of a stunning solo run, with Everton’s goalkeeper out of position following a late corner. Dowman’s achievement surpassed the long-standing record previously set in 2005 by **James Vaughan**, who scored at 16 years and 270 days old.

This landmark moment was only Dowman’s third Premier League appearance, yet it underlined his remarkable rise at Arsenal, where he has also broken youth records and impressed in academy football. Manager **Mikel Arteta** described the occasion as “magical,” highlighting the teenager’s composure and technical ability on one of English football’s biggest stages.
