# Nick Faldo Demands Tiger Woods Accountability After DUI Arrest

Golf legend **Sir Nick Faldo** has publicly urged that **Tiger Woods** be held accountable for his actions following the American golfer’s recent DUI arrest and car crash in Florida. Faldo sharply criticized both Woods’ behavior and the **PGA Tour’s response**, calling it “predictably weak” and saying that serious consequences are necessary.  

Faldo acknowledged the sympathy many feel for Woods — who has dealt with chronic pain and injuries throughout his career — but emphasized that this sympathy must be balanced with responsibility and accountability, especially given the severity of the incident. He stressed that the sport should not just “care for Tiger” but also ensure that actions have repercussions.  

The comments come in the wake of bodycam footage released by police showing Woods’ arrest after his vehicle rolled in Jupiter Island, Florida. Woods has pleaded not guilty to charges including driving under the influence and has since announced he is stepping away from golf temporarily to seek treatment and focus on his well‑being.  

Faldo also took aim at the response from golf authorities, suggesting that simply offering support without clear consequences sends the wrong message — especially to younger fans and aspiring players.  

Supporters of Woods, including fellow legends like **Jack Nicklaus**, have expressed concern for his health, but Faldo’s remarks highlighted that accountability should be part of any response to such a serious incident.
