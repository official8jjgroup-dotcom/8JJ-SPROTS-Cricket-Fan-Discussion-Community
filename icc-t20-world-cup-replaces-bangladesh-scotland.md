# [ICC T20 World Cup Replaces Bangladesh with Scotland](https://8jj.com/icc-t20-world-cup-replaces-bangladesh-with-scotland/)

The **ICC T20 World Cup 2026** has seen a major development as **Bangladesh** has been officially replaced by **Scotland**.  

This decision comes after nearly **three weeks of talks** with the **Bangladesh Cricket Board (BCB)**, which expressed **unwillingness to play in India** due to security concerns. The ruling ensures that **Scotland** will now compete in the tournament, marking a significant change ahead of the global event and drawing widespread attention from fans and analysts alike.
