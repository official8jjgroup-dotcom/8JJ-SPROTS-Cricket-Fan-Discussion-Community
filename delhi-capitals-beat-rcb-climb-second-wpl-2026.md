# [Delhi Capitals Beat RCB To Climb Second In WPL 2026](https://8jj.com/delhi-capitals-beat-rcb-to-climb-second-in-wpl-2026/)

**Delhi Capitals** secured a dominant **seven-wicket win** over **Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB)** to move into **second place** in the **WPL 2026** standings.  

After opting to field, DC produced a **clinical bowling performance**, bowling out RCB for just **109 runs**. They then chased down the target with **26 balls to spare**, handing RCB their **first defeat of the season** and showcasing their growing dominance in the league.
