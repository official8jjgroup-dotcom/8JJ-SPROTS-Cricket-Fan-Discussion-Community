# Purple Cap Race: 5 Big Changes as Krishna Climbs

The **IPL 2026 Purple Cap race** is heating up, with several significant changes in the standings. Among the biggest stories is the rise of **Prasidh Krishna**, who has climbed the leaderboard with a string of impactful performances.

## 1. Krishna’s Impressive Surge

Prasidh Krishna has emerged as one of the standout bowlers this season. His ability to pick up wickets at crucial moments has helped him move rapidly up the Purple Cap standings, putting him firmly in contention.

## 2. Tight Race at the Top

The competition for the Purple Cap remains extremely close, with only small differences separating the top bowlers. This means the leaderboard can change quickly with just one strong performance.

## 3. Key Role of Pressure Overs

Bowlers who perform well during high-pressure phases — particularly the powerplay and death overs — are gaining an edge. Krishna’s success has largely come from delivering in these crucial moments.

## 4. Emerging Players Making Impact

While experienced bowlers continue to perform, younger players like Krishna are stepping up and challenging established names, adding excitement to the competition.

## 5. Plenty of Matches Still to Come

With many matches left in IPL 2026, the Purple Cap race is far from decided. Consistency will be key for any bowler hoping to finish at the top.
