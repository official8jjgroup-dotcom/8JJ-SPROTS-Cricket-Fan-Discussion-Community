### 📊 Impressive Domestic Record  
One of the biggest reasons behind this support is Green’s strong domestic numbers. He holds a **first-class batting average above 45**, rising to over 50 in the Sheffield Shield — a key indicator of his potential. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}  

### 🔄 All-Round Value Remains Crucial  
Green’s ability to contribute with both bat and ball makes him a valuable asset. Even when not fully fit to bowl, his role as a batting all-rounder adds balance to the Australian lineup.

### ⚠️ Workload and Injury Management  
Currently recovering from a back issue, Green’s workload is being carefully managed — including restrictions during IPL 2026 — to ensure he is fully fit for upcoming international assignments. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}  

### 🔮 Long-Term Vision
