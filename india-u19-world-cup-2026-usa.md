The ICC has announced the fixtures for the **U19 Men's Cricket World Cup**, scheduled from **January 15 to February 6**, with the final to be played in **Harare**.

The U19 World Cup 2026 follows four groups, a **Super Six stage**, two semi-finals, and the final. The groups are:
